About

Flux is written and maintained by Ritchie Turner. I provide consultancy and programming services around haXe, Node.js and the Flux framework.

I have experience in C, Java, Javascript, Python and PHP. Currently i am studying Erlang as inspiration on direction for Flux and OCaml due to it's intersection with haXe. I also like Lisps, particularly Clojure (the ideas of Rich Hickey are fantastic), and I do automation scripts in Newlisp as it's small and portable.

I'm well versed in 'full spectrum' web development from Linux server setup; bash, postgresql, redis, couchdb, nginx etc to client tech (dojo, jquery etc).

I prefer:

  • Linux to Windows
  • Emacs to IDEs
  • Static to dynamic typing
  • Json to XML
  • Async to threads

I enjoy writing tools and looking for better abstractions. My current interests lie in Bitcoin, and learning and applying functional paradigms/style to my work.

If you wish to use my services i charge the equivalent of 2 ounces of gold in your currency per month. I accept partial payment in Bitcoin.

I live in Santiago, Chile, with my wife and baby daughter.
